Our Story

It all began with a chance encounter—one of those moments where the stars align, and life takes an unexpected yet beautiful turn. We met in High School, and what started as a simple conversation quickly turned into something much more. Before we knew it, we were inseparable.

As we spent more time together, we realized that our connection was something truly special. We've laughed until we've cried, supported each other through life's ups and downs, and created countless memories that we'll cherish forever. Whether it's driving through Germany at midnight, or just cozy nights in, every moment we've shared has brought us closer together.

Fast forward to 2024, and here we are—planning our wedding and looking forward to the next chapter of our lives. We're so excited to celebrate our love with you, our family and friends, who have been with us every step of the way.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We can't wait to share this special day with you and create even more memories together!

Questions? Answers.

  • When is the RSVP deadline?

    We kindly ask that you RSVP by October 5th & respond only for yourself and any plus ones noted on your invitation. We appreciate your timely response and can't wait to celebrate with you!

  • Where should we park?

    The Colony House has parking onsite (no fee or valet required).

  • Is there a dress code?

    We're embracing the beauty of the season with an autumnal formal dress code. We invite our guests to join us in rich, warm tones inspired by fall—think deep reds, earthy browns, elegant golds, and burnt oranges. We can't wait to celebrate with you in style!

  • Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

    The officiant will prompt a moment for personal pictures or video. After that time frame, we invite you to be truly present with us during our ceremony & kindly ask that you turn off all devices.

  • Will alcohol be served?

    Yes! We will have an open bar throughout the event, so you can enjoy a variety of drinks. Cheers!

  • Is there a gifts registry?

    We are fortunate to have most things we need for life and love. With that said, we know some people prefer to show love through gifts, for which we have a registry. We created ours with WithJoy. Either way, we would appreciate your prayers and well wishes as we start this next chapter together.
