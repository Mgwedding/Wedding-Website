Questions? Answers.

  • When is the RSVP deadline?

    We kindly ask that you RSVP by October 5th & respond only for yourself and any plus ones noted on your invitation. We appreciate your timely response and can't wait to celebrate with you!

  • What is the best way to RSVP?

    Please RSVP by October 5th by clicking on the RSVP buttons on this website (or click here). You will be able to RSVP using the first and last name listed on the invitation and your additional guest(s) should populate. Please answer a couple of questions before submitting your response.

    If you have any questions, please reach out to us directly!

  • Can I bring additional plus ones or my children?

    While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adult-only event. We hope this will be a special evening for you to relax and enjoy!

  • Where should we park?

    The Colony House has parking onsite (no fee or valet required).

  • Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

    The officiant will prompt a moment for personal pictures or video. After that time frame, we invite you to be truly present with us during our ceremony & kindly ask that you turn off all devices.

  • Will there be meal options for guests with dietary restrictions or allergies?

    When RSVPing, please note any dietary restrictions or allergies & we will try to work with the venue to provide alternative options.

  • Will alcohol be served?

    Yes! We will have an open bar throughout the event, so you can enjoy a variety of drinks. Cheers!

    Please plan to arrive at least 15-30 minutes before the ceremony begins (starts at 3:30 PM) to allow time for parking and seating.
